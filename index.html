Privacy, spelled out

We read your chats so Phrased can help.
We read them your way.

Phrased is an app that looks at your screen. That's a big ask — so we built it to deserve the trust. Here's what actually happens, in the app's own vocabulary.

"Do you send my screenshots to your servers?"
Phrased No. Your phone takes the screenshot and extracts the text locally. The image never leaves your device.
"So what does the AI actually see?"
Phrased Just the text of the last few messages — enough to understand the vibe, nothing more. Sent once to generate the reply, then discarded.
"Are my conversations stored anywhere?"
Phrased Nowhere. Nothing is logged, saved, or tied to your account. The AI has no memory between sessions — every reply starts from scratch.
"What about the chats I upload for voice training?"
Phrased Only your extracted phrases are kept — the raw screenshots are processed and then deleted. You can wipe your voice profile from Settings anytime.

We're a small team. We read our own privacy pages. If something's unclear, email us — we'll fix the copy, not the policy.
